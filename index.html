Our Applications

BNPP

The Bahamas National Patient Portal is a user-friendly digital platform designed to enhance communication between patients and healthcare providers.

Learn more

BEMR+

BahamasEMR+ is the country’s national digital health record system, designed to streamline patient care and improve data-driven decision-making across public health services.

learn more

Metabase

Metabase is a business intelligence platform that enables users to explore and visualize data through interactive dashboards and queries.

learn more

EIRs

Electronic Immunization Registries (EIRs) securely track individual vaccination records, helping healthcare providers manage immunization schedules, monitor coverage, and improve vaccine delivery and public health outcomes.

Learn more

SIP+

The SIP+ is a specialized platform designed to collect, manage, and analyze data related to maternal and newborn health, supporting improved outcomes for mothers and babies through evidence-based care and monitoring.

learn more
